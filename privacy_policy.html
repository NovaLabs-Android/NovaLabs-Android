Nova Labs Privacy Statement

Date: June 21, 2018

Nova Labs respects and is committed to protecting your privacy. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our privacy policies, and how we collect, use and disclose information about you that we receive from you and from others.

This Privacy Policy describes our practices in connection with information that we collect through software applications (including mobile applications).


Crashlytics - Crash Log Reporter

We collect crash logs related to our app that occur on your device. The crash log contains non-personal information that includes your device's brand and model, operating system name and version, and the stacktrace error that caused the crash.


Google AdMob

In order to help support further development of the app, we may include in-app advertisements provided by Google's AdMob network. As per Google's own privacy policy, personalized data collected about you is used to deliver ads that are more relevant to your interests. If opting out of personalized data usage, you will instead receive non-targeted ads.


Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.


Who can I contact if I have questions about this Privacy Policy?

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
